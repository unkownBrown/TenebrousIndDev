About Tenebrous Indo Games

Our Mission

Tenebrous Indo Games is dedicated to showcasing the best indie games from Indonesian developers. We provide a platform for talented creators to share their work with gamers worldwide.

Our goal is to promote Indonesian game development and help local developers reach a global audience.

Indonesian Culture in Games

Many of our games incorporate elements of Indonesian culture, folklore, and landscapes. From the mystical temples of Java to the beautiful beaches of Bali, our games bring Indonesia to life.

We believe games can be a powerful medium for sharing culture and telling unique stories.
